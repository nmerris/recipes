In a large bowl, mix together 2.5 cups AP flour, 0.5 cup whole wheat flour, 1 tsp yeast, 1 tsp salt, 1/2 diced fresh sweet onion, a bunch of fresh dill, and some caraway seeds.  Add 1.5 cups lukewarm water.  Mix just until combined, do not knead!  Let sit tightly covered for 8-24 hours at room temp.  Preheat large dutch over at 450 F.  Dust outside surface of dough with flour, bake in dutch oven for 30 min, take off lid and bake another 15 min.  Let cool for at least 15 min before serving.
